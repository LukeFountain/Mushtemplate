Located in the Innovation Barn!

Mush! is committed to producing delicious mushrooms and mushroom products while minimizing waste at our farm and in our community. Mushrooms, and the complete organisms known as fungi, are the principal decomposers in ecological systems and we are utilizing the successes of nature to close the waste loop on human activities. We have partnered with Envision Charlotte to bring Mushroom Power to the Innovation Barn, Charlotte's living lab for the circular economy. We are currently repurposing wood shavings from Carolina Urban Lumber (another Innovation Barn tenant) to produce gourmet and medicinal mushrooms for purchase. Come visit us onsite, online, or at our next event!
